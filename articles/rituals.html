Don’t just follow the ritual—discover the reason!!!

Let’s understand where we are today with Hinduism and its practices through a simple story.

Once, in a peaceful monastery, a teacher and his students used to meditate every evening. But there was a cat that kept making noise and distracting everyone. So the teacher decided to tie the cat up before meditation. It worked—everyone could focus better. Over time, tying the cat became a daily ritual. Years passed. The teacher died, the students left, and even the cat passed away. But new students came—and guess what? They brought in a new cat just so they could tie it up before meditation, without ever asking why it was done!

This is where many of us are today—following rituals without understanding their deeper meaning. It's time to look beyond habits and uncover the true wisdom behind the practices!
